Our Journey of Impact

Follow our story from the first book drive to our latest project, chronicled step-by-step.

FEBURARY 2025

Kanye, Botswana

This book drive collected over 500 books, which were used to create a dedicated children's section in a public library in Kanye. The space is now filled with colorful storybooks and early readers.

OCTOBER 2024

Phalombe, Malawi

Throughout the summer of 2024 we collected more than 500 books and raised $550 from selling candy and crowd-funding. All of the books were donated to the Ramokgonami Primary School while the money was used to pay for the domestic/international shipment of the books in Botswana for the community!

JUNE 2024

Buduburam, Ghana

Through the months of January to May of 2024 we collected more than 600 books and raised $520 from selling candy. All of the books were donated to the Addai Memorial in Ghana for the community! The following link will take you to their facebook page.
