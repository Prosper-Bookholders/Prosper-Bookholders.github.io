Changing Lives, Library by Library.

At Prosper Bookholders, we collect books and donate these books to other countries. Books we donate are used to build libraries in schools or public spaces. We have been operating since 2024!

About Us

Prosper Bookholders is an organization dedicated to building libraries in countries around the world. We have established relationships with libraries and communities around the Dallas area through our ventures. Us volunteers at bookholders are dedicated to helping those who can't always help themselves. Prosper Bookholders is run completely by high schoolers and is indepdent from the Prosper ISD district.
